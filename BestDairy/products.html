Pure Ghee

Ghee has a good amount of Fat & Energy. From centuries, we are using it as a partial Medicine. Oue way to make Ghee is
(1) Sour raw milk is churned into butter. The butter is boiled in an open pan to allow the water to evaporate. The hot ghee is transferred and stored.
(2) Milk is separated into cream which is then churned into butter. The butter undergoes heat clarification to produce ghee.
We are providing it in loose quantity as you need.
